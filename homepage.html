1. ARE WE REALLY LOCKED IN THE ROOM?

Spoiler alert!
All our escape rooms are equipped with emergency exit buttons.


2. ARE YOUR ESCAPE ROOMS SCARY?

Our escape rooms are intended to be spooky. Our rooms are designed to be immersive, suspenseful and challenging, but our intention is never to purposely scare you.


3. DO WE HAVE TO PLAY WITH STRANGERS?

All our escape games are private experiences. You will not be paired up with strangers. Once you book an escape room it is reserved exclusively for you and your teammates.


4. IS THERE A DRESS CODE?

There is no strict dress code to play our escape rooms, but you should dress for comfort and for movement.


5. HOW MANY ROOMS ARE THERE IN EACH GAME?

Part of the adventure is to discover this with your team mates—so, sorry, you’ll have to play to find out.


6. WHAT CAN I BRING INTO THE ESCAPE ROOM?

All you need is your favourite teammates, your inner detective and puzzle solving skills! We provide lockers free of charge for storing phones, cameras, purses, etc.
(Sorry, no recording devices are allowed in our theme rooms.)


7. ARE YOUR ESCAPE ROOMS PHYSICALLY DEMANDING?

Our escape games are designed for you to use your wits, and not meant to be physically demanding! You may need to bend over or lift small things but No force is required, just mental strength!


8. WHAT ARE THE DIFFICULTLY LEVELS OF YOUR ROOMS?

All of our themes are designed to have the same level of difficulty, and are meant to be extremely challenging.
Each theme consists of different styles of puzzles, in order to challenge all of your mental faculties.
